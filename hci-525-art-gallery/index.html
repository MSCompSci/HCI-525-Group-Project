Michelangelo Virtual Art Gallery

Welcome!

This virtual art gallery invites you to travel to Italy from the comfort of your own home and interact with Michelangelo's renowned sculptures and paintings from 1498-1512. While inside the virtual gallery, select a piece of art to learn more about it. You'll be able to use the keys on your keyboard and mouse to move around and view the space around you! If you would like to return to the website, simply click the 'esc' botton and click where you would like to go.
